About

I Can't Believe It's Gluten-Free is a gluten-free bakery, specializing in home made from scratch gluten-free baked goods that don't taste gluten-free. All of our baked goods are gluten-free and rice-free. Some are even nut and dairy-free. Our unique blend of flours is the secret to keeping our baked goods moist and delicious. You won't get any of that dry, grainy stuff here.

When you taste our goodies you won't believe they are gluten-free. Order something from our wide variety of goodies for your next gathering or just to satisfy your sweet tooth. Either way, you will be glad you did. Even your non-gluten-free friends will love them, so be sure to order extras.

All baked goods are made to order, and available for local pick up (Lake Orion, MI) or delivery (additional charge).
